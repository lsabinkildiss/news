Communications @ Moffat

The Moffat Library of Washingtonville publishes two email newsletters a month that both outline our news and inform of upcoming programs, events and services at the library.

The library also publishes a printed newsletter of our full quarterly listing of programs. This is mailed to all library district residents at the beginning of September, January and April.

Don't miss out on all our upcoming programs! Be the first to learn about what's new @ Moffat.
